African Lion

Two distinctive features set lions apart from all other wild cats. First, they are social felines while other cats are typically solitary in the wild. Second, they are the only cats to exhibit a physical distinction between males and females— males have a mane.

Family Matters

Lions live in prides of between four to 37 cats. Prides usually consist of a core four to 12 related adult females, their offspring and one to six adult males. In the pride, females do most of the hunting, when they work together to form an ambush.
