About

AR Zoo

provide an interactive and educational experience for users, showcasing various animals and their habitats, without the need for physical travel

Accessibility

To make the zoo experience accessible to a wider audience, including people with disabilities or those living far from a physical zoo.

Sustainability

To reduce the environmental impact of zoos, by reducing the need for physical travel and reducing the carbon footprint of zoos.

Conservation

To raise awareness about endangered species, their habitats, and the need for conservation efforts, by providing educational and engaging content about the animals

Engagement & Guidance

To provide an immersive and interactive experience, allowing users to explore and learn about different animal species in a fun and engaging way.
